Amrhein et al. (2018), Tierney et al. (2020), and Annan et al. (2022)
SST/SIC boundary conditions for the LGM. Note that SIC in the Annan 
boundary condition is copied from Amrhein.

Also includes boundary conditions representing first and fourth
quartiles of ensemble members from the LGMR and Annan 
reconstructions. Ranking of ensemble members is based on estimated
net feedback (using CAM5 Green's functions). See Methods.
